Literally in Greek we ask "how do you have?".

ἕχεις is 'you have' in Greek if you're talking to one person. πῶς is 'how'.

To answer with "I'm doing ___" we literally say "I have ___".

ἔχω means 'I have'.

'well' or 'good' is καλῶς.

How would you say "I'm doing well"?

How would you say "I'm doing well"?

  • ἔχω καλῶς.

'bad' or 'poorly' is κακῶς.

How would you say "I'm not doing well"?

ἔχω κακῶς.
